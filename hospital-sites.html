Healthcare & Clinic Websites

A series of high-accessibility, trust-focused digital platforms engineered for healthcare providers and clinical specialists.

The Requirement

Healthcare platforms demand a specialized UX strategy: they must establish immediate clinical trust and ensure critical information—like booking availability and specialist profiles—is discoverable within seconds.

Strategy & Execution

I prioritized AA accessibility standards and implemented calming, trust-evoking color palettes. The engineering process focused on extreme performance optimization to ensure fast loading speeds for users who may be accessing the site in urgent situations.

Outcome

Significantly improved the patient journey by streamlining the inquiry and appointment booking processes, resulting in higher digital engagement for the clinical teams.
