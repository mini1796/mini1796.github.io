Hospital & Clinic Websites

A series of accessible, trust-focused websites for healthcare providers.

The Requirement

Healthcare websites require a different approach: they must build trust immediately and make information (like booking times and doctors' profiles) extremely easy to find.

My Role & Process

I focused on accessibility and calming color palettes (blues and greens). The development prioritized fast loading speeds for users potentially accessing the site in emergencies.

The Outcome

Improved patient experience by simplifying the appointment inquiry process.
