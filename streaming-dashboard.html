Streaming Service Analysis

A comparative Power BI dashboard analyzing Netflix, Disney+, and Prime Video.

The Goal

To analyze vast datasets of user behavior across major streaming platforms and visualize the competitive landscape.

My Role & Process

I cleaned the raw dataset and used Power BI to create comparative visualizations. I focused on "Data Storytelling," using color coding to differentiate platforms and clear charts to show market share and content popularity.

The Outcome

A clear visual breakdown of user demographics and content preferences across the streaming industry.
