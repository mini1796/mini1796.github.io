Streaming Service Analysis

A comparative Power BI dashboard analyzing Netflix, Disney+, and Prime Video to visualize the competitive landscape.

The Goal

The objective was to analyze vast datasets of user behavior across major streaming platforms. I focused on "Data Storytelling," using platform-specific color coding and clear, comparative charts to highlight market share and content popularity.

Process & Outcome

I cleaned the raw dataset and used Power BI to create interactive visualizations. The final result provided a clear visual breakdown of user demographics and preferences, helping to identify key market trends.
