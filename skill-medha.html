SkillMedha CMS Platform

A complex, multi-portal system for students, colleges, and corporate partners.

The Challenge

This massive project required three distinct user portals (Students, Colleges, Corporates). The main challenge was creating a unified Design System that could support complex dashboards for admins while remaining simple enough for students.

My Role & Process

I built a comprehensive component-based Design System in Figma. This included designing role-based access control interfaces and complex data tables. I worked closely with developers to ensure the Figma components translated perfectly to code.

The Outcome

The design system reduced design-to-development handoff time by 30% and ensured a consistent brand identity across all three portals.
