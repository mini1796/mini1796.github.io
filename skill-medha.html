Skill Medha: Placement Automation

A dual-facing EdTech platform architected to automate placement workflows and skill-building for students and recruitment partners.

The Role & Vision

I designed the platform's entire interface and served as a core developer to build the placement automation and skill-building system. My role involved directing the complete visual design strategy in Figma, from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes for both students and recruitment partners.

Dynamic Resume Builder

Automated tool for students to create professional, system-compatible resumes.

Recruiter Portal

Centralized dashboard for partners to manage candidate pipelines efficiently.

Core Features

Student Dashboard

Comprehensive student overview featuring skill analytics, drive status, and personalized learning paths.

Recruiter Portal

Centralized hub for recruitment partners to manage candidate pipelines and evaluation workflows.

Drive Management

End-to-end automated system for tracking placement drives from announcement to final hiring.

Student Learning Platform

Integrated learning environment featuring an AI-powered chatbot to assist students with real-time doubt resolution and navigation.
