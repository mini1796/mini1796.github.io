Topic Prep: Govt Exam Platform

A comprehensive government exam preparation platform architected to optimize student learning, retention, and engagement through gamified workflows.

The Role & Architecture

I architected the entire platform from initial wireframing and prototyping in Figma to the final frontend deployment. My focus was on improving student retention through an intuitive navigation system for massive content libraries, including multi-tier course structures and sub-topic hierarchies.

Library Navigation

Engineered a multi-tier navigation system to handle complex course hierarchies.

Mobile First

Optimized the assessment experience for one-handed mobile usage.

Figma Wireframes & Prototypes
Complex Sub-topic Navigation

To drive daily usage, I implemented several gamified UI elements that directly impacted user behavior:

  • Streak Trackers: Visual daily targets that encouraged consistent study habits.
  • Achievement Badges: Milestone-based rewards that gamified the learning process and celebrated progress.
  • User Flow Analysis: Continuous refinement of navigation paths to ensure students could access materials with minimal friction.
