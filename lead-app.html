Lead Management System

A mobile-first CRM designed for field sales teams to track, update, and manage leads with zero friction.

The Problem

Field sales agents were losing track of customer details because existing tools were too complex for mobile use. They needed a rapid-input system that could capture interaction context in under 10 seconds while on the move.

User Research & Flow

Through shadowing sales reps, I identified that "thumb mapping" was critical. Agents needed to operate the app with one hand while commuting. The resulting flow prioritizes quick-action buttons and semantic colors over complex text statuses.

Final UI & Prototyping

The final interface uses high-contrast cards and intuitive status indicators. I built a hardware-accelerated prototype in Figma to test the swipe physics and gesture tracking.
