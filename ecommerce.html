E-Commerce Brand Store

An end-to-end, conversion-focused online shopping experience designed for a premium dry fruits brand.

The Goal

The client required a fully functional, high-performance online store that simplified the purchasing journey. The focus was on creating a secure, visually rich platform that minimized friction and maximized conversion rates.

End-to-End Implementation

I managed the full lifecycle of this project. Starting from high-fidelity UI designs in Figma focused on product visibility, I developed the frontend using semantic HTML, CSS, and modern JavaScript. To finalize the store, I integrated the Razorpay payment gateway for secure, real-time transactions.

The final product is a fast, responsive, and intuitive shopping experience that handles everything from complex cart management to secure, encrypted checkouts seamlessly.
