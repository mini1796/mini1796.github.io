News + Chat + LMS Super App

A comprehensive "Super App" integrating news feeds, real-time chat, and learning modules into a unified experience.

The Challenge

Users were suffering from "app fatigue," needing to switch between three different platforms to stay updated, communicate, and study. The client wanted a singular, cohesive ecosystem that resolved context-switching friction.

Information Architecture

I conducted extensive card sorting exercises to map out the hierarchy. We prioritized "Chats" and "News" as the primary landing actions, while the LMS modules were placed under a dedicated "Learn" vertical accessible via the persistent bottom navigation.

Interactive Prototype

The final UI features a white-space heavy layout to offset the high cognitive load of consuming varied content types. The prototype was built in Figma to validate the seamless transitions between article reading and instant messaging.
