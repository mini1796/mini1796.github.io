E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

An interactive data dashboard to track KPIs and operational metrics.

The Requirement

The client needed a clear way to visualize complex sales and operational data to identify bottlenecks in their product lifecycle which were previously hidden in spreadsheets.

My Role & Process

I used Power BI to ingest raw data and transform it into interactive visual reports. I applied UI design principles to the dashboard layout to ensure key metrics were scannable and easy to understand at a glance.

The Outcome

The dashboard highlighted key inefficiencies, enabling the management team to adjust their strategy and reduce operational waste by 20%.
