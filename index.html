About

Resume LinkedIn GitHub

I'm Yamini Sappa — a Frontend Software Engineer and UI/UX Designer who works at the intersection of product design and scalable web development.

Over the past 4+ years, I’ve designed and built production-grade digital products including learning platforms, assessment systems, and enterprise dashboards used in EdTech environments. My work spans the entire product lifecycle — from wireframing and high-fidelity UI design in Figma to building responsive, high-performance interfaces using modern frontend technologies.

Across multiple platforms, I’ve designed 40+ product screens, developed complex UI systems, and implemented modular component architectures that support scalable product growth.

What differentiates my work is the ability to operate across both design and engineering layers. I can craft a pixel-perfect interface while also architecting the frontend logic that powers it. This dual perspective allows me to move quickly from concept to production while maintaining usability, performance, and visual consistency.

My focus is on building intuitive, scalable product experiences where thoughtful design and efficient engineering come together.
