TEJ: Current Affairs Reels

A high-fidelity mobile experience designed for rapid micro-learning, featuring a fast-paced 'reels' style feed optimized for performance.

The Role & Vision

As the Lead End-to-End Designer, I conceptualized and managed the entire visual and interaction lifecycle of TEJ. This included wireframing, high-fidelity UI design in Figma, and defining the motion design language for smooth vertical scrolling and seamless transitions.

Core Features

Interactive Feed UI Mockup
Gesture tracking Visualization
  • Micro-Learning Focus: Designed the interface for rapid content consumption, allowing students to stay updated in seconds.
  • Engagement Triggers: Defined interactive triggers and gamified elements to increase session duration and retention.
  • Optimized UX Flow: Conceptualized a resource loading and playback strategy to ensure a frictionless user experience on mobile.
