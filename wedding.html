Wedding Invitation Website

A personal creative project combining web design and video animation.

The Idea

To create a unique, memorable invitation for my sister's wedding that went beyond a traditional paper card.

My Role & Process

I designed a responsive single-page website containing venue details and the couple's story. I also created an animated invitation video using advanced PowerPoint animation techniques.

The Outcome

A delightful digital experience that guests loved and found incredibly useful for getting venue directions and updates.
