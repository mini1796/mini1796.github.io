E-Commerce Inventory Management

A robust backend web application engineered to track stock levels, orders, and product listings with automated reorder intelligence.

The Problem

Managing stock levels across a growing online store using manual spreadsheets led to frequent overselling and stockouts. The client needed a centralized "Command Center" to automate vendor communication and maintain high data integrity.

Operational Workflow

I mapped out a non-linear workflow: The system continuously monitors stock levels → Generates "Low Stock" alerts → Pre-fills reorder forms based on historical vendor data → Tracks incoming shipments until they are reconciled with the physical inventory.
